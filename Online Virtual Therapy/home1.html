FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How does online therapy work? Online therapy, also known as virtual therapy, allows individuals to connect with licensed therapists through video calls, chat messaging, or phone sessions from the comfort of their own space.
Is online therapy effective? Numerous studies have shown that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person therapy for many mental health concerns. It offers convenience and accessibility without compromising the quality of care.
How do I choose a therapist on soulEase? soulEase provides a platform where users can browse through licensed therapists based on their specialty, availability, and user reviews. Clients can choose a therapist that best fits their needs and preferences.
Is my information secure on soulEase? soulEase prioritizes client confidentiality and privacy. The website employs encryption and other security measures to ensure that sensitive information shared during therapy sessions is safeguarded.
Can I schedule appointments at my convenience? Yes, soulEase offers flexibility in scheduling appointments. Clients can choose the date and time that works best for them, making therapy accessible and convenient for individuals with busy schedules.
